# Polyhedra


## Overview

|                | Tetrahedron    | Octahedron     | Hexahedron     | Dodecahedron   | Icosahedron    |
|----------------|----------------|----------------|----------------|----------------|----------------|
| Faces          | 4              | 8              | 6              | 20             | 12             |
| Vertices       | 4              | 6 (2 × 3)      | 8              | 12 (4 × 3)     | 20 (8 + 4 × 3) |
| Schläfli symbol | {3, 3}         | {3, 4}         | {4, 3}         | {5, 3}         | {3, 5}         |
| Vertex Configuration | 3.3.3     | 3.3.3.3        | 4.4.4          | 5.5.5          | 3.3.3.3.3      |
